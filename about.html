Our Story

Founded in 2081 BS, Amanshree Gharelu Udhyog began as a small family business which is first time in Rajbiarj, sapatri, with a vision to provide high-quality paving solutions to our community. What started as a modest operation has grown into a trusted name in the construction industry across Nepal. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs.

From our humble beginnings in Rajbiraj, we've expanded our operations while maintaining our core values of integrity, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. Every paver block we manufacture carries the legacy of our founder's vision and the hard work of our dedicated team.

Today, we take pride in being one of the most reliable paver block manufacturers in the region, serving both residential and commercial clients with products that stand the test of time.
